How it works

Simulate a battle between teams consisting of Pokémon, Yu-Gi-Oh! cards, and DND monsters. Choose a team for yourself and a team for the enemy and see who comes out on top!

Choose your team

You can use teams made by anyone in the community, or you can create your own team. It's reccomended to go over the character roster to see who you want to add to your team.

Showdown!

On the showdown page, select your team and then the enemy's team. Upon entering the battle, choose each team member's target and the move they should use. Beware, the enemy wont go down without a fight!
